Bags

There are various types of Bags:

  • Channel: Chanel handbags, particularly the iconic flap bag, are known for their timeless design, meticulous craftsmanship, and enduring style, featuring quilted leather, a signature CC turn-lock closure, and a chain strap.
  • Louis Vuitton: Louis Vuitton bags are renowned luxury handbags and accessories, known for their iconic monogram canvas, exquisite craftsmanship, and timeless designs, including popular styles like the Speedy and Neverfull.
  • Gucci: Gucci handbags come in a range of sizes and styles. They are small or medium-sized, made of leather, canvas, and suede, and feature zippered compartments and metal locks or magnetic snap closures. Some have adjustable straps, usually made of leather.
